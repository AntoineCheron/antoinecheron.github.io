Hi, I'm Antoine

I am an Inria Ph.D. student trying to solve the scientific problems that prevent us software engineers from building hypermedia APIs, which will lead us to an even more automated and fun world.

What do I do ?

hypermedia as the engine of application state

As a Ph.D. student, I'm aiming at solving the scientific problems that still keep developers away from building hypermedia API, 17 years after Roy Fielding presented HATEOAS in his thesis.

Why it matters ?

At Zengularity, it's been more than 4 years that we design and build hypermedia APIs for our clients.

These APIs open up a whole lot of new possibilities, usages and automations such as automated testings, type safety, automated composition of services, etc.

Now we need to solve some scientific problems in order to make building hypermedia API easy.

What I'd like to achieve during my Ph.D.

Any kind of help is welcome

  • Push a standard to W3C
  • Have major contributions in the academic area (ICSE, ...)
  • Have major contributions in the industry (Scala IO, Scala days, Devoxx, ...)
  • Bring tools to developers to ease the development of hypermedia API
  • Demonstrate how hypermedia API can be a game-changer in the future of the web industry
